# Stories

*The roots beneath the fruit.*

Not every journey ends with a deliverable work — but every journey is a story, and every story is worth telling. Stories are the creative process itself: the spark, the dead ends, the unexpected turns, the moments of confusion, and the small joys of understanding.

A work without a story is hollow. A story that never becomes a work is still whole.

This is where we record those stories. Some will grow into works. Some will remain here, complete as they are — because the making matters as much as the made.

---

## How Stories Work

A story can be:
- A **creation journal** — documenting the process of making something, step by step, including the failures
- A **learning narrative** — the path through a question, with all the wrong turns left in
- A **collaboration record** — how people (and AI) worked together on an idea
- A **fragment** — a spark that hasn't found its shape yet, but deserves to be remembered

Stories naturally link to [scenes](../scenes/), [knowledge](../knowledge/), and [works](../works/). A story may give birth to a work. A work should always point back to its story.
