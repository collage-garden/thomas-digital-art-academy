## 🎧 Binaural Beats Explorer

**Live**: [collage-garden.github.io/binaural-beats](https://collage-garden.github.io/binaural-beats/) · **Repo**: [collage-garden/binaural-beats](https://github.com/collage-garden/binaural-beats)

An interactive tool for experiencing binaural beats — the auditory illusion where the brain perceives a rhythmic pulsation from two slightly different frequencies delivered to each ear. The beat does not exist in the physical sound wave; it is a neural product.

Three audio modes (Pure Tone, Music SSB, Drone), four frequency distribution strategies, real-time beat envelope visualization, PWA offline support. Pure frontend, zero dependencies.

**Scenes**: [Auditory Art](../scenes/auditory-art/) · [Interactive Installation](../scenes/interactive-installation/) · [Social Observation](../scenes/social-observation/) · [Everyday Life](../scenes/everyday-life/)

**Knowledge touched**: Physics (acoustics, wave interference) · Mathematics (FFT, Hilbert transform, signal processing) · Neuroscience (Superior Olivary Complex, binaural processing) · Psychology (perception bias, placebo) · Philosophy (epistemology — how do we know what we know?)

**What makes it a Collage Garden work**:
- It started from a real question, not a definition (Charter II)
- The [engineering journey](https://github.com/collage-garden/binaural-beats/tree/main/docs) documents 8 evolutionary steps — including the wrong turns (Charter I, III)
- Three satirical editions (Science / Marketing / Mystic) use the same audio engine with different narratives — proving that packaging shapes perception (Charter III)
- AI co-created, transparently labeled (Charter VII)
