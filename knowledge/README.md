# Knowledge Fragments

*The toolkit, not the curriculum.*

This is where disciplinary knowledge lives — organized systematically so you can find what you need, but never mistaken for the purpose of this garden. These fragments exist to **serve creation**, not to govern it.

You don't come here to "study physics." You come here because you're making something and you need to understand why a string vibrates the way it does. You take what you need and go back to your work.
