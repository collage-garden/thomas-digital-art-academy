# Auditory Art

*Sound, music, acoustics, and the experience of listening.*

This path explores how sound is made, shaped, perceived, and felt — from the vibration of a bamboo flute to the mathematics of harmony to the neuroscience of why music moves us.

---

## Works

### 🎧 [Binaural Beats Explorer](https://collage-garden.github.io/binaural-beats/)

When each ear receives a pure tone at a slightly different frequency, the brain perceives a rhythmic pulsation that does not exist in the physical sound wave. This tool lets you experience that phenomenon firsthand — and then asks a harder question: does the same sound feel different when wrapped in different stories?

Three audio modes (Pure Tone, Music SSB, Drone) explore the phenomenon from different angles. Three satirical editions (Science, Marketing, Mystic) demonstrate how narrative reshapes perception of identical stimuli.

**Knowledge in play**: Physics (acoustics, wave superposition) · Mathematics (FFT, Hilbert transform) · Neuroscience (Superior Olivary Complex) · Psychology (perception, placebo)
