## Works

### 🎧 [Binaural Beats Explorer — Satire Editions](https://collage-garden.github.io/binaural-beats/?edition=marketing)

The same audio engine, three completely different experiences. The Science edition presents objective facts. The Marketing edition wraps identical sound in pseudo-scientific buzzwords and fake neural stats. The Mystic edition invokes chakras, kundalini, and remote blessing.

The sound is identical. What changes is the story. This is an observation about how packaging shapes perception — and about the wellness industry, pseudoscience marketing, and the gap between what is said and what is real.

**Knowledge in play**: Economics (how bullshit is monetized) · Psychology (placebo, framing effects) · Philosophy (epistemology, rhetoric vs. truth)
