# Everyday Life

*The art and science hidden in daily routines.*

This path explores the extraordinary within the ordinary — how physics, mathematics, psychology, and design shape the world we move through every day.

---

## Works

### 🎧 [Binaural Beats Explorer](https://collage-garden.github.io/binaural-beats/)

You put on headphones every day. But do you know what happens when each ear hears a slightly different frequency? Your brain invents a beat that isn't there. This tool lets you hear it — and then shows you how the same sound can feel completely different depending on what story surrounds it.

A daily reminder that perception is not passive. We don't just receive the world; we construct it.
