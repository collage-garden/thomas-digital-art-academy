# Garden Paths

*Six intersecting paths through the garden.*

They cross freely — a project may live at the intersection of two or three scenes.

---

### 🌿 [Everyday Life](everyday-life/)

The art and science hidden in daily routines

### 🔭 [Social Observation](social-observation/)

How societies work, how people interact, how cultures evolve

### 🎵 [Auditory Art](auditory-art/)

Sound, music, acoustics, and the experience of listening

### 🎨 [Visual Art](visual-art/)

Image, form, color, space, and the experience of seeing

### 🕳️ [Interactive Installation](interactive-installation/)

Where technology meets physical space and human participation

### 🎭 [Traditional Art](traditional-art/)

Ancient craft through modern eyes
